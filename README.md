**Funzi**

Funzi is a fun and educational app made with Flutter.
It helps kids learn by playing simple and colorful games.

**Sound and Image Match**

Listen to a sound (like an animal or object).
Choose the correct picture that matches the sound.

**Coloring Game**

Pick any sketch and color it freely.
Use different brush sizes and colors.
Erase and redraw anytime you want.
Save your coloring to continue later.

**Picture and Shadow Match**

Match each object with its correct shadow.
Helps kids recognize shapes and outlines.

**Emotion Game**

Learn about emotions using faces and expressions.
Match the correct emotion with the right picture.

**Object Identification Game**

A question is asked along with 3 pictures
Kids tap the correct image based on the question.

**Odd One Out**

Three pictures appear — two are similar, one is different.
Kids must find the odd picture.

**Puzzle Game**

2×2, 3×3, or 4×4 puzzle pieces.
Pieces are shuffled — drag and place them to complete the picture.

**Highlights**

Supports both English and Arabic.
Simple design, made for kids.
Fun sound effects when you complete levels.
Confetti animations for correct answers.
Works on both Android and desktops.
All data (like drawings) are saved automatically.
Offline — no internet required once installed.
